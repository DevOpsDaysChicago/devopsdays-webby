**Abstract:**

Code is cool, the 'Internet of Things' is cool, but have we
lost sight of who we are supposed to be serving - The Internet of People?
By exploring the relationship between leading edge quantum physics,
technology, and human behaviour, the audience will be challenged as
follows:

*Are your skills with 'things' matched by your skills with 'DNA'? *
