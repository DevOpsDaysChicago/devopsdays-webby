# Security and Continuous Delivery

## Abstract

We will share our success stories and lessons learned on working toward Continuous Delivery on a public facing web application for a Government Agency. This will cover Infrastructure Engineering, Build and Release Engineering, End to End Auditability and Tracability,  1 Click Application Deployments, and Security from the Infrastructure to the Application Workflow.

## Speaker
Sriram Narayanan, Principal Consultant at [ThoughtWorks](http://thoughtworks.com/).

Prasanna K is a Penetration Tester at [ThoughtWorks](http://thoughtworks.com/).

## Bio

Sriram "Ram" Narayanan ([@sriramnrn](https://twitter.com/sriramnrn)) is a Principal Consultant at ThoughtWorks, and help clients in their Continuous Delivery journey - covering application architecture, change management, development and testing practices, build, infrastructure and release engineering, and Infrastructure security.

Prasanna K a.k.a Shifu is a Penetration Tester at ThoughtWorks, and helps teams ensure that their application workflows are secure and not vulnerable to attacks.
