DevOpsDays is coming to Oslo!

<%= render(:partial => "/#{@eventhome}/_target_date") %>

For the first time, DevOpsDays is coming to Oslo! DevOpsDays is a self-organizing conference for DevOps practitioners. It brings together leaders in Software Development and IT Operations to collaborate and learn. It is both a technical conference and a conference focusing on culture, processes and structure within organizations. We encourage both technologists and business people to attend, learn and share experiences.

At a high-level, each day will consist of a mix of presentations, workshops, ignite presentations, and open spaces. There will be an evening party for all participants scheduled for the evening of September 5th. A great opportunity for networking, mingling and having a good time.
