**Abstract:**

We've developed an eight step incident plan for quickly resolving outages.

I'd like to go over the plan, and illustrated using an actual outage.

1. Breathe
2. Scope
3. Communicate
4. Pick your team
5. Fix it.
6. Communicate
7. Post-mortem

**Speaker:**

John Boxall is CTO at Mobify.
